Frequently Asked Questions

  • Are you a licensed private investigator?

    Yes. I am fully licensed by the California Bureau of Security and Investigative Services (BSIS), License #013773. You may verify my status at search.dca.ca.gov.

  • Do you carry liability insurance?

    Yes. I maintain both general liability and professional Errors & Omissions (E&O) insurance to protect clients and ensure compliance with industry standards.

  • What is your professional background and area of specialization?

    With a foundation in law enforcement, I specialize in administrative investigations—such as personnel reviews, officer-involved incidents, and internal affairs–style inquiries—for public agencies, school districts, and municipalities.

  • Are you available outside of regular business hours?

    Yes. Investigations often require flexibility. I offer evening, weekend, and on-call availability to meet the demands of each case.

  • Will my case be handled confidentially?

    Absolutely. All case files and communications are maintained under strict confidentiality protocols. Nothing is disclosed without your written authorization or valid legal compulsion.

  • Who will handle my case?

    I personally manage and execute every aspect of your investigation. You will have direct access to me throughout the engagement.

  • Will you coordinate with my attorney?

    Yes. I routinely work alongside legal counsel and can tailor my reporting and evidence presentation to support legal proceedings.

  • How often will I receive updates?

    Update frequency is customized to each client’s needs—daily briefs, weekly summaries, or real-time alerts are all available based on case requirements.

  • Will I receive a formal report?

    Yes. At case conclusion, you will receive a comprehensive written report, including timelines, findings, and any photographic or documentary evidence collected.

  • Do you use any illegal or unethical methods?

    No. All investigative activities comply with California law, BSIS regulations, and professional ethical standards. I never resort to unauthorized surveillance or deceptive practices.

  • Are you available to testify in court if needed?

    Yes. I am experienced in providing expert testimony and will appear in court or administrative hearings to present my findings when required.

  • Do you use a written contract?

    Yes. A clear, written agreement detailing scope, fees, confidentiality, and terms is provided before any work begins.

  • What are your fees?

    Services are billed at an hourly rate plus approved expenses. A retainer is typically required, and a detailed estimate is provided in advance.

  • Can you guarantee the result I’m hoping for?

    No. While I commit to conducting a thorough, lawful investigation, ethical practice prohibits promising specific outcomes. My responsibility is to deliver accurate, objective findings.
